TabLift Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2026-07-16

If you signed into TabLift using Google or Apple, this page explains how to permanently delete your TabLift account and all data associated with it — whether you use the browser extension or the Android app.

Option 1: Delete it yourself, instantly, in the extension or app

In the browser extension:

  1. Click the TabLift icon in your browser toolbar.
  2. Expand the Account section.
  3. Sign in if you aren't already.
  4. Click "Delete my account…", then confirm.

In the Android app:

  1. Open the TabLift app and go to Settings.
  2. Sign in if you aren't already.
  3. Tap "Delete account", then confirm.

This immediately and permanently deletes your account and every piece of data tied to it — there is no grace period or soft-delete. If you have an active Pro subscription, it's cancelled first so you won't be charged again.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you no longer have the extension installed, email trav-dev@outlook.com from the email address associated with your account (or tell us which provider you signed in with) and ask us to delete your TabLift data. We'll complete the deletion within 30 days and confirm by email.

What gets deleted

  • Your account identity (email address and account ID)
  • Your synced settings and search API key
  • Your synced song library

If you never created a TabLift account, none of your data was ever sent to us in the first place — everything lives only on your device, and uninstalling the extension removes it.

See the full Privacy Policy for more detail on what we store and why.
